The Self-managing Anycast Networks for DNS (SAND) project aims at developing solution(s) for supporting the complex management of anycast DNS. The project is a consortium consisting of: SIDN and SIDN Labs, NLnet Labs, and University of Twente. Project timeline: Abril-2018 to Mar-2020 - Phase3 Nov-2016 to Nov-2017 - Phase2 Nov-2014 to Nov-2016 - Phase1
